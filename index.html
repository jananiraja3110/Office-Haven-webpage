About Us

Building technology that powers businesses

OfficeHaven is a full-spectrum technology partner offering infrastructure solutions, IT hardware, custom software development, and product engineering — all under one roof.

Our Infrastructure team handles server setup, cloud migration, and network architecture. Our Hardware division provides refurbished and new IT equipment on lease or purchase — desktops, laptops, servers, and peripherals.

On the development side, our Software Development team builds web apps, mobile apps, GenAI-powered solutions, and enterprise tools tailored to your business. Our Product Development practice takes ideas from concept to market-ready product with UI/UX design, MVP builds, and launch support.

Whether you're a startup needing your first infrastructure stack, an enterprise scaling hardware across offices, or a business launching a new digital product — OfficeHaven delivers.
